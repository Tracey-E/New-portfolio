PONTOON

The goal of Pontoon is to get the cards to add up to 21 without going over 21. The person/computer with highest score wins
Play=start game. Twist= deal 1 more card. Stick = see who has the highest card values. New game = clear the board and start again.
J,Q,K all = 10.
Aces can either = 1 or 11
Click play to begin

SNAP

The goal of Snap is to collect all the 52 cards into your hand by clicking the SNAP button when the numbers or letters on the cards match if a player runs out of cards first the other player wins.
make sure the snap button is visible on your screen

Clock

The goal in Clock is to fill all of the outer clock numbers before you get four kings in the center.
To start your game click Begin and then the Turn button above the K(ing)
the red hand will show you which card to turn next.
if you find a king there will be no red line so use the turn button above the K(ing).
This game is best played at 80% zoom level
